The blog is a research about coronavirus(COVID-19) in Belgium from March 2020 to August 2020 and used the data source from Kaggle and used the dataset from package coronavirus.

You can visualise the number of COVID-19 cased confirmed in Belgium from March to August 2020 in Figure 1. It shows that April was the most seriousness month that have the largest infected population. Since then, people started pay attention and took anti-epidemic action and the confirmed cases got a good result. However, there are still have high number of confirmed cases.

For further research, it found that the elder got higher infected rate, Figure ?? listed confirmed cases in difference provinces and regions and difference age group. It shows that the largest number were from 80-89 age group in every region in April.
